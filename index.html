About IIIT-Bangalore

Set up in the year 1999, and promoted by the Karnataka Government and the IT Industry, IIIT-Bangalore attracts quality students every year from all over India and abroad, to its acclaimed iM.Tech, M.Tech, MS by Re- search and Ph.D programmes. The institute has a short yet distin- guished history of producing high quality post-graduate engineers every year.

The 1700+ strong alumni occupy key positions in a number of corporations, delivering tremendous value to their organi- zations. Students from different technical and cultural backgrounds come together in the institute, realizing the potential of unity and teamwork. Immense support from the wonderful faculty enables the students to stay on the path of scientific enquiry and together, they have produced several patents, published in influential journals and conferences and carried out innovative projects.
